About the PME Equipment Database

The PME Equipment Database is a inventory list maintained by PME lab managers to provide business partner with the latest information of scientific equipments available at the Pritzker School of Molecular Engineering.

Search Equipments

Search by keywords

In the search box at the top of the search page, type in keywords and press search. The database will present equipments that have name or overview containing the keyword

Search by categories

  1. Hover over the list of categories at the left of the search page;
  2. Select one or more categories. Selecting multiple categories will show the equipments belonging to either of the selected categories;
  3. Refine your selection by clicking on the selected categories under the list of categories;
  4. Press "Submit" to search or "Clear" to clear your selections.

Clear selections

To reset selections and search results, click the "Clear" button under categories or click the logo at top left.
